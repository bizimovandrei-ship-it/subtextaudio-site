About

A boutique studio for voice, sound and localization.

Founded by Andrey Bizimov, Subtext Audio is a remote audio production studio delivering AI voiceover, narration, podcast production, YouTube audio and localization for Russian and international clients.

Founder

Andrey Bizimov brings 15 years of radio experience, including hosting an hour-long information program and producing weekly advertising audio programs.

Production background

The studio draws on more than a decade of audio editing practice—cleanup, pacing, structure, voice direction, mixing and publication-ready delivery.

Current focus

Subtext Audio specializes in AI voiceover, audio production, YouTube narration, podcast production, localization and multilingual audio for remote international projects.

Studio model

AI-assisted production with human oversight.

We work studio-first: AI extends speed and voice range, while professional review, editorial quality control and sound judgement shape the final result.
