About

A compact audio studio for voice, sound and meaning.

Subtext Audio is founded by Andrey Bizimov as a remote audio production studio for AI voiceover, narration, podcast production, YouTube audio and localization.

Founder

Andrey Bizimov brings 15 years of radio work, including hosting an hour-long information program and producing weekly advertising audio programs.

Production background

The studio is built on more than 10 years of audio editing practice: cleanup, pacing, structure, voice direction, mixing and delivery-ready files.

Current focus

Subtext Audio now specializes in AI voiceover, audio production, YouTube narration, podcast production, localization and digital services for remote international projects.

Studio model

AI tools with human editorial control.

The brand stays studio-first: AI helps with speed and voice range, while editorial judgement, sound quality and project context guide the final result.

Available for projects

Remote audio production for creators, teams and companies. For staging inquiries, write to subtextaudio@gmail.com.
